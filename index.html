Personal Training

Should I hire a personal trainer?

Hiring a personal trainer is essential. The benefits are remarkable as they go a long way to guarantee that you reach your health and fitness goals. Some people initially get turned off to hiring a personal trainer when they reflect upon the cost or the fear of a trainer pushing them too hard.

We support, motivate, and guide you through this transformative journey.

Our training includes:

  • Paying close attention to individual training programs and their nutrition habits such as customized programs
  • Arranging fitness assessments to measure strength and imbalances
  • Addressing fitness level and desired goals
  • Accountability
  • Form correction
  • Weekly flexibility and strength plans
  • Nutrition advice and goal setting
  • Unconditional professionalism to help you progress in developing a healthy lifestyle

Full Bodyweight Workout

The most effective way to boost your metabolism, burn body fat, gain strength with a variety of movements that work the major muscle groups. This class is for all fitness levels.

Core and More

This circuit style class will move you in different directions to improve balance, core stability, posture, fall prevention and improve cardiovascular fitness. This class is for all fitness levels.

Mat Pilates

Strongly core centric, Pilates is about lengthening, strengthening, and stability. Every movement originates from the core. Benefits include increased joint mobility, flexibility, balance, strength and endurance, and improving posture. Grip socks and a mat are recommended.

Total Body Pump

A circuit style class, Pump is a dynamic, metabolic workout designed to combine aerobic activity with strength training, burning fat and increasing muscle strength and endurance. This is a more challenging class but with modifications still appropriate for all levels.

Aqua Aerobics

A total body class focusing on cardio and strength. Benefits include improved balance and coordination, core stability, increased metabolism, less impact on joints, improved posture, and decreased stress. It's fun, social, and ideal for everyone. Aqua shoes required.

About

Our passion is to give others their lives back

Heather had a dream of having her own Training business. Her vision is to help clients be successful in their training by providing excellent customer service, passion, and knowledge. Our business, JOY Thru Fitness, started six years ago.

We are Traveling Trainers passionate about helping clients of all ages and ethnicities live healthier lifestyles. Our intent behind traveling to you is that convenience is half the battle!

JOY Thru Fitness enjoys training youth to older adults. Our clients enjoy training with us because of our ethics, transparency, knowledge, and passion for helping others. We believe in being transparent, punctual, and ethical when making decisions.
